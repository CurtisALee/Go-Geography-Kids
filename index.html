North America

The country is located within the continent known as
North America, bordering both Canada (north)
and Mexico (south).

From East to West, the distance is roughly 2,092 miles,
not including Alaska or Hawaii, which aren't apart
of the mainland.

Population

With a country as large as the U.S, the vast cultural
differences that span across the nation allow for some
amazing cultural hubs.

From the Jamaican districts of New York, the Latin restaurants
of Miami and proud French and African communuties of the
South, America is definitely as diverse as they come.

Washington D.C

Weather

Due to the geographical location of the country,
interesting weather patterns have developed across
the mainland, such as the tropical heat on the South East,
up to the famous Tornado Alley.

Hurricane Katrina was one of the worst hurricanes in U.S
history, destroying New Orleans in 2005.
